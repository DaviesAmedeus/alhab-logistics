Who We Are....

Alhab 001 Logistics is a prominent logistics and transportation provider operating in Tanzania. Backed by more than ten years of solid experience in the logistics industry, we deliver dependable, efficient, and customized services across the supply chain—from inland transportation to warehousing and freight forwarding.

Our strong understanding of the Tanzanian and East African logistics environment, combined with a customer-focused mindset, makes us a trusted partner for organizations in various sectors, including FMCG, manufacturing, oil & gas, and government institutions.
